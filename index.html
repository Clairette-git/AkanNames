AKAN NAMES.

Akan names are derived from Ghanian culture.Frequently in Ghana, children are given their first name as a 'day name' which corresponds to the day in the week they were born. These "day names" have further meanings concerning the soul and character of the person. Middle names have considerably more variety and can refer to their birth order, twin status, or an ancestor's middle name.

Female Names

  • Sunday: Akosua
  • Monday: Adwoa
  • Tuesday: Abenaa
  • Wednesday: Akua
  • Thursday: Yaa
  • Friday: Afua
  • Saturday: Ama
